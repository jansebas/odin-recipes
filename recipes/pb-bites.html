3-Ingredient Peanut Butter Bites

Description

Made in less than 15 minutes, these gluten-free 3-Ingredient Peanut Butter Bites are a wholesome, make-ahead snack option or tasty lunchbox addition.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups rolled oats
  • 2/3 cup peanut butter
  • 3 tablespoons honey

Steps

  1. Add the oats to a food processor and blend until oats reach a flour consistency.
  2. Add the peanut butter and honey. Blend until ingredients are well combined and come together to form a dough ball. May need to scrape sides once or twice.
  3. Roll into tablespoon-sized round bites. If dough isn't holding together, add 1-2 tbsp. more of peanut butter.
